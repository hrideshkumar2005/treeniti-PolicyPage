3. Data Sharing

  • Trusted services (hosting, analytics)

4. Data Security

We use secure systems and encryption to protect your data. However, no system is 100% secure.

5. Data Retention Policy

We retain your user data only for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide our services. Different types of data are kept for different periods:

  • Account Information: Your name, email address, and login details are retained for as long as your account remains active. Upon receiving an account deletion request, this data is permanently deleted from our active databases within 30 days.
  • App Activity & Progress: Your tree growth, rewards, and in-game achievements are stored to keep your profile active. This data is deleted when your account is deleted.
  • Optional Location & Media Data: Any optional location or uploaded images are kept only as long as necessary to provide the relevant features and are removed when you delete them or close your account.
  • System & Device Logs: Technical log data (such as IP addresses and device info) is stored temporarily for security, troubleshooting, and fraud prevention purposes, and is automatically deleted or anonymized within 90 days.

If you request account deletion (via the form below or by contacting us), we will permanently erase all your personal data from our servers. Please note that some transaction logs or support communications may be retained for longer periods to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, or enforce our agreements.
